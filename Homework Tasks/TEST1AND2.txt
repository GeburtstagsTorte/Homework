TEST:
1.
In: series of numbers composed of n values
Out: True if that series is a permutation of the series {1, 2, 3, ..., n}
     False otherwise

example:
In: 2, 6, 1, 4, 3, 5
Out: True because the input is a permutation of {1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6}


2.
In: x, y and series of numbers
Out: the numbers in the series which are inside the interval (x, y), displayed in reversed order of how they appear in the series

example:
In: 
    x = -10
    y = 9
    12, -4, 9, 7, -5, 31, -74, 515, -33, -8 (from file)
Out: -8, -5, 7, -4
